About me

My full name is Enkhtushig Namkhai and I am a 4th year at University of California Berkeley. I am a Computer Science major that really loves being creative. I am very interested in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and how we can design devices for the target users by simply repeating the design cycle. In my free time I love doing Graphic Design and applying these same principles to my work. One of my aspirations in life is to travel around the world and get exposed to all the different kinds of people and cultures that exist. I love making international connections and lifelond friends!
