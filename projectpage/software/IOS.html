HappyBird:

This game was made by following a tutorial that can be found here. Although I used the basic ideas and the graphics, after I understood the idea, I tried coding it myself so I ended up changing a lot of the code. I am still working on the project. In the future I want to make it harder as the player gets a higher score. Right now all the pipes get spawned with random heights. There are a total of 6 pipes (3 top and 3 bottom) that get repositioned once they are off screen.
