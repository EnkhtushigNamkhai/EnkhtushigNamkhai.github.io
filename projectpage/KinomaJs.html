Calorie Converter:

A short programming assignment for cs160 (User Interface Class). How many bananas equals a plate of spaghetti? A quick programming assignment which, given a type of food and the quantity of that food, will be able to see other types of food and quantity with an equivalent number of calories. For example, let's say in the application you enter 2 plates of spaghetti, which is 600 calories per item. When convert is clicked you should be able to see how many bananas 2 plates of spaghetti are equivalent to. For more detailed documentation of how it works, please go here.

XKCD Comics:

What is XKCD? It's only the most relevant comic known to this world. With just stick figures and simple drawings, XKCD can strike at the heart of any matter. For this assignment, you will be building a XKCD visualizer in a Kinoma platform of your choice (mobile or web). You will use XKCD's API to fetch comics and display them in your app. Additionally, you will use the Flickr API to pull an image that relates to the title of the XKCD comic and display that.
