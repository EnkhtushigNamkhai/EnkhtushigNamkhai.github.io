Christmas Robot

In our Electrical Engineering 40 class, we created a Christmas robot as our final assignment. The song is hard coded into the MSP430 device. We tried to put more songs in it so that we could switch between songs, however, the MSP430 did not have enough memory to allow us to do this. In the process, we had to buy a 12V step up component in order to increase the voltage from 3.3V to 12V to light the LED strips. Although the memory space and 3.3 Volts limited us to do cooler things, it was still very fun! This is one of the coolest things I have worked on and it is also my first project that I have made from scratch! You can watch our project demo HERE.

Grant High School Renovation Project

During my senior and junior year of high school, our school won a million dollars to reinnovate the promanade in our school thanks to our Environmental Design teacher, Mrs.Tavares. Before the award was granted, I was taking her Environmental Design class, and in this class she made us do a lot of interesting assignments. We were able to participate in this real life project by designing it. She taught us the basics of Photoshop and CAD to get us started, and had us measure, label, and design everything in the promanade. It was a really special project because some of the designs that we though of, got incoporated into the place. And to see the designs that we thought about actually come to life was really amazing.
