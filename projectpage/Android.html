Android Projects

Criminal Intent:

A project that I made using Android Studio by following the guide from the Android Programming book by Bill Phillips, Chris Stewart, Brian Hardy and Kristin Marsicano. "Criminal Intent records the details of 'office crimes' - things like leaving dirty dishes in the breakroom sink or walking away from an empty shared printer after documents have printed. With CriminalIntent, you can make a record of a crime including a title, a date, and a photo. You can also identify a suspect from your contacts and lodge a complaint via email, Twitter, Facebook, or another app. After documenting ad reporting a crime, you can proceed with your work free of resentment and ready to focus on the business at hand. " For more detailed documentation please go here.
