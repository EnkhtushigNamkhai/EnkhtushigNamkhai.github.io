My Travels at Mongolia

This is where it all started for me. I was born in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia and moved to the US in 2004. In the year of 2012, we decided to go back to Mongolia and visit it once more. It had been 8 years since I didn't go back to Mongolia or meet my grandparents and relatives, so going back was a really memorable experience for me.
